\hypertarget{regression-tables-for-racial-resentment-models}{%
\section{Regression Tables for Racial Resentment Models}\label{regression-tables-for-racial-resentment-models}}

Table \ref{tab:chamb-rr} presents the regression results for the racial resentment models using OLS. Table \ref{tab:chamb-rr-logit} presents these results using a logistic regression. There are fewer observations in these models than in Table 3 in the body of the paper and Table \ref{tab:dist-logit} because not every district in the country was home to a respondent to the CES. Although the relationship between racial resentment and probability of sponsorship is not statistically significant in the upper chamber model after controlling for other covariates (\emph{p} = 0.109) the relationship is substantively large. Taken with the other results presented in the body of this article and the SI, I conclude that racial resentment probably is associated with sponsorship above-and-beyond the other covariates, the statistical nonsignificance of this one coefficient notwithstanding.
